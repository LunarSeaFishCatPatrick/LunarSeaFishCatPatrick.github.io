Nahida

Element: Dendro

Weapon: Catalyst

Role: Support

Nahida is a wise and compassionate character from Genshin Impact. As the Dendro Archon, she is deeply attuned to the natural world and its harmony. Her abilities as a Dendro character make her an exceptional support unit who enhances her allies' potential in battle.

Nahida is revered for her intelligence, empathy, and connection to Sumeru's wisdom. As a wielder of the Dendro element and a catalyst, she combines her profound understanding of nature with her supportive skills to empower her team and protect the balance of the world.
