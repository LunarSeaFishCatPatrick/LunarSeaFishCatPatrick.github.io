Arlecchino

Element: Pyro

Weapon: Polearm

Role: DPS

Arlecchino, also known as "The Knave," is one of the Fatui Harbingers. Fierce and commanding, she leads the House of the Hearth, an orphanage that produces loyal Fatui agents. Her ideals of strength and unwavering obedience drive her mercilessly, making her one of the most feared figures within the Fatui ranks.

Arlecchino is driven by an unrelenting belief in absolute loyalty and personal strength. As the overseer of the House of the Hearth, she raises abandoned children with strict discipline and loyalty to the Fatui cause. Despite her cold and ruthless nature, she has a twisted sense of care for the orphans under her charge. Her actions and ideology make her one of the most complex and morally ambiguous characters among the Harbingers.
