Navia

Element: Geo

Weapon: Claymore

Role: DPS/Support

Navia is a charismatic and kind-hearted character from Genshin Impact. As a dedicated protector of justice, she is deeply connected to her homeland and its people. Her abilities as a Geo character make her a powerhouse in battle, capable of both defense and offense.

Navia is known for her confidence, charm, and unwavering sense of duty. She acts as a beacon of hope and resilience in her region. As a wielder of the Geo element and claymore, she combines her strength with her compassion to protect her allies and uphold justice.
