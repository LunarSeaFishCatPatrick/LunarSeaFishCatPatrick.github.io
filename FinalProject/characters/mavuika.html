Mavuika

Element: Pyro

Weapon: Claymore

Role: Dps/Sub-DPS

Mavuika, the enigmatic healer of the seas, is a Hydro wielder known for her serene demeanor and unwavering dedication to protecting her allies. Her mastery of water-based healing arts makes her an indispensable member of any team.

Mavuika's abilities are deeply tied to the ocean's tranquility and power. She channels the essence of water to heal and protect, while her calm and composed nature inspires those around her. Despite her gentle exterior, she harbors a fierce determination to safeguard her loved ones.
