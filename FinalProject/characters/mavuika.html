Mavuika

Element: Hydro

Weapon: Polearm

Role: Healer/Support

Mavuika is a serene and wise character from Genshin Impact. She has a profound connection with water and uses her Hydro abilities to heal and support her team in battles.

Mavuika is a calm and composed individual who values balance and harmony. As a wielder of the Hydro element and a polearm, she combines her precise attacks with powerful healing abilities to keep her allies safe and strong.
