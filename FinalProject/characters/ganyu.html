Ganyu

Element: Cryo

Weapon: Bow

Role: DPS

Ganyu is a quiet and gentle character from Genshin Impact. As the secretary of Yuehai Pavilion, she is known for her dedication and grace. Her Cryo abilities make her a powerful DPS character.

Ganyu is a half-qilin adeptus who bridges the human and adepti worlds. Her Cryo element and bow allow her to deal devastating damage from afar, making her one of the most sought-after DPS characters.
