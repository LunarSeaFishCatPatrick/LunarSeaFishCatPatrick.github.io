Xilonen

Element: Dendro

Weapon: Catalyst

Role: Healer

Xilonen, the gentle guardian of nature, wields the power of Dendro to heal and protect her allies. Her connection to the natural world allows her to nurture life and restore balance.

Xilonen is a skilled healer who uses her Dendro abilities to restore health and provide support in battle. Her calm and nurturing presence makes her a vital part of any team, ensuring her allies can fight without fear.
